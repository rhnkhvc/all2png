About Us

Our Mission

At All2PNG, our mission is to provide a fast, secure, and entirely client-side image conversion tool. We recognized that users often hesitate to upload sensitive documents or high-quality images to third-party servers due to privacy concerns and potential data breaches. That's why we built a solution that brings the power of file conversion directly to your web browser.

Why Choose Us?

Our platform utilizes modern web technologies to process your files locally. This means your data never leaves your device. Not only does this ensure 100% privacy, but it also allows for lightning-fast conversions without waiting in server queues or dealing with upload limits.

  • Privacy First: No files are uploaded to our servers.
  • High Quality: We use advanced algorithms to ensure lossless conversion.
  • Free to Use: Our tools are completely free, with no hidden fees or subscriptions.
  • Universal Accessibility: Works on any modern browser, across all platforms.
