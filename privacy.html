Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At All2PNG, your privacy is our highest priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our conversion tools.

1. Client-Side Processing (No File Uploads)

Our core promise is 100% security. All2PNG is a client-side web application. This means that when you convert a file (such as a PDF, WebP, SVG, or JPG to PNG), the entire conversion process happens locally within your web browser using your device's memory.

We do not upload, transmit, store, or view your files. No files are ever sent to our servers.

2. Information We Collect

Because our tool operates locally, we do not require you to create an account or provide personal information. We use standard analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand aggregated traffic patterns and improve the website. This data does not contain any reference to the files you convert.

3. Advertising

We use third-party advertising companies (such as Google AdSense) to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.

4. DMCA & Copyright Policy

We respect the intellectual property rights of others. Since All2PNG operates purely as a client-side tool and no files are uploaded to our servers, we do not host, store, or distribute any user-converted content. Therefore, it is impossible for us to remove specific files from our non-existent databases. Users are solely responsible for ensuring they have the appropriate rights and permissions to modify any files they convert using this browser tool.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at admin@all2png.com.

Disclaimer: All2PNG is a client-side tool. By using this service, you acknowledge that you are responsible for any copyright or licensing rules pertaining to the files you convert. We do not store, view, or distribute your files.
